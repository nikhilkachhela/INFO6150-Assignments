Brew Guide

Dial in your method with ratios, grind sizes, and timing notes.

Pour Over

  • Ratio: 1:16
  • Grind: Medium-fine
  • Time: 3–3:30

Bloom 30–45s, then pour in pulses to maintain a steady dome. Aim for a flat bed.

French Press

  • Ratio: 1:15
  • Grind: Coarse
  • Time: 4–5:00

Stir after the initial pour, steep, then plunge gently to avoid over-extraction.

Auto Drip

  • Ratio: 1:17
  • Grind: Medium
  • Time: Machine-dependent

Use fresh filters, rinse the basket, and keep your machine clean for best flavor.

Espresso

  • Ratio: 1:2
  • Grind: Fine
  • Time: 25–32s

Distribute evenly, level your puck, and adjust grind to hit the target time.

FAQ

Do I need a scale?

Highly recommended for consistency. Ratios are your friend.

Whole beans vs pre-ground?

Whole beans, ground just before brewing, give the best results.

How do I store coffee?

Airtight container, cool and dark. Avoid the fridge.
